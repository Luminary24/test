Planning Framework

Your Complete Budget Guide.

Real numbers across every tier. Sequence your destinations intelligently and never be surprised by costs again.

The biggest travel mistake is budgeting from hope instead of data. You pick a number that feels comfortable, then spend your trip either anxious about overspending or realising you packed too conservatively to enjoy yourself.

This framework gives you researched, real numbers across three tiers for every destination — so you can build a trip that genuinely fits your style, with no surprises and no mental maths (the live currency converter handles that).

Daily Cost Tiers
🎒
Budget Backpacker
$25–$65/day
Hostel dorms, street food, slow travel. Best in Asia and Latin America.
  • Dorm beds or cheap guesthouses ($6–15/night)
  • Street food and market meals ($1–4/meal)
  • Buses, trains, shared transport
  • Free attractions, beaches, hikes
  • Night buses = free accommodation
🧳
Mid-Range Traveller
$75–$175/day
Private rooms, sit-down restaurants, mixed transport. Comfortable worldwide.
  • Private hotel or Airbnb ($35–90/night)
  • Sit-down restaurants ($10–25/meal)
  • Occasional short-haul flights
  • Guided day tours and paid activities
  • Monthly Airbnb for 30–50% savings
✦
Luxury Traveller
$250–$600+/day
Boutique hotels, fine dining, private transfers. Accessible everywhere.
  • Boutique hotels and 4–5 star resorts
  • Fine dining and premium experiences
  • Business/premium class flights
  • Private tours and exclusive access
  • Concierge-level planning throughout
4-Month Sample RTW Route

The Bestseller Circuit

The most popular first-time RTW route — sequenced to start cheap, hit the global highlights and end strong.

Est. total: $5,000–$9,000 mid-range · $3,500–$5,500 budget · International flights not included

Phase 1 · Weeks 1–5
🌏 Southeast Asia Base
Thailand → Vietnam → Bali. Cheapest region on Earth. Find your travel legs here, meet other travellers, spend very little.
Budget: $25–40/day · Mid: $55–85/day
Phase 2 · Weeks 6–9
🇯🇵 Japan Deep Dive
Japan alone deserves 3–4 weeks. More expensive but incomparable. Capsule hotels and convenience stores keep it manageable.
Budget: $65–90/day · Mid: $110–160/day
Phase 3 · Weeks 10–13
🏛 Southern Europe
Portugal → Spain → Italy or Greece. Move slow. Monthly Airbnb destroys accommodation costs. Best food on Earth.
Budget: $60–85/day · Mid: $110–165/day
Phase 4 · Weeks 14–17
🌎 Latin America Finale
Mexico → Colombia → Peru. The most exciting travel region right now. Budget-friendly, culturally rich. End at Machu Picchu.
Budget: $30–55/day · Mid: $65–110/day
